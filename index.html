About

I am a Doctoral Researcher at Tecnológico de Monterrey, where I work on computer vision, deep learning and medical image processing. I did my MPhil at Shah Abdul Latif University, where I was advised by Dr. Munsif Ali Jatoi. I did my MSc at the University of Sindh, where I was advised by Dr. Farhat Naureen Memon. During my MSc degree, I received the Vice Chancellor's silver medal for standing first in the department.
